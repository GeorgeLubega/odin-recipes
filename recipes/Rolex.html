ROLEX

referred to as Rolex, is a popular food item in Uganda, combining an egg omelette and vegetables wrapped in a chapati.

This single-portion dish is quick to prepare, and can be eaten at any time of the day, from breakfast to a lunch or supper meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • chapati
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Put one tablesppon full on a pan
  2. wait until heated then add Eggs
  3. Then put your chapati on top
  4. Cut Tomatoes and Onions and add on top
  5. Roll to make a Rolex
